Welcome to Farm Wars

Welcome to Farm Wars, an Ataxx clone with supercowpowers!

Your objective: take over the board! Move your farm animal horizontally or diagonally along the grid. A one-space move will split your animal, while a two-space move will make it jump. Land next to an opposing player's piece, and those pieces will be absorbed (kinda like Othello).

The game is won once the board is filled, or when all of one player's pieces have been absorbed.

Enjoy!
